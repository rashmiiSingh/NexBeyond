FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Find answers to common inquiries about the ecosystem.

NexBeyond is India's most practical, project-driven AI learning ecosystem. It is engineered for students, software engineers, and professionals who want to transition from passive AI users into active AI builders by writing, reviewing, and deploying real code rather than just watching lecture slides.
While foundational coding knowledge (e.g., basic Python or JavaScript syntax) is helpful, we structure our learning modules to scale in complexity. We begin with core concepts and work up to advanced agent coordination, model fine-tuning, and production system design.
Our mentoring is code-level and synchronous. You receive detailed feedback on pull requests, cohort code reviews during weekly reviews, 1-on-1 career mapping alignments, and architecture critiques from senior engineers and authors like founder Rashmi Singh.
You will build production-grade, multi-agent frameworks, fine-tuned localized language models, vector search indexes (such as Pinecone or Qdrant), and full-stack software assemblies that integrate and run models dynamically.
We partner with high-growth AI startups, technology firms, and venture capitals. As you complete and deploy projects on your builder dashboard, your profile and verified repositories are directly matched and recommended to hiring panels.
